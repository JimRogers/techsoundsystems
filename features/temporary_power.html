TEMPORARY POWER

All events however small require a stable power supply. Tech Event Services can provided temporary power supplies tailored to your needs. Working along side generator rental companies, TES can provide all types and sizes of generators, 24/7 power and fuel management. TES work closely with event organisers to ensure power is distributed correctly. All work is installed to BS7909 (temporary electrical installations)
