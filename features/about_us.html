ABOUT US

Tech Event Services was formed in 1986 by Julian Kemp specialising in 100 volt line public address systems, as time went on concert sound and lighting was added to the ever growing hire stock to meet the demands of the customers. In 2000 Julian’s son Adam joined the company and now working together they provide a range of facilities needed for all occasions. Over the past 30 years the company have supplied many of the events on the south coast from small fates to major sporting events.

    Our services now include

  • Outside public address systems
  • Concert & Theatrical Sound
  • Concert & Theatrical Lighting
  • Temporary Power
  • Two way Radio’s
  • Conference systems
  • Production management
  • Temporary flood lighting
  • Installations
